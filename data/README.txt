Format of the files are:
Samples results are stored in log*.txt
jumps measutements are stored in hist*.txt 

For every given sampling duration "t", bandwidth "bw" :
log_t_bw  --- contains the sampling data for "t" seconds with "bw" bandwidth
hist_t_bw --- contains the histogram of jump measuremnts for the usecase of (sampling for t seconds with bw bandwidth)
